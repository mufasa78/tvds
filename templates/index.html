Traffic Violation Detection System

This system uses YOLOv8 and DeepSORT algorithms to detect and analyze traffic violations near zebra crossings.

Main Functions:

  • Lane line identification
  • License plate recognition
  • Illegal line crossing detection
  • Pedestrian right-of-way violations

How It Works

Our system combines state-of-the-art computer vision algorithms to detect and analyze traffic violations:

Object Detection: YOLOv8 identifies vehicles, pedestrians, and road features
Object Tracking: DeepSORT tracks objects across video frames
Violation Detection: Our algorithms analyze movement patterns and detect rule violations
Reporting: Generate detailed reports with screenshots, timestamps, and violation types
Note: Video processing may take several minutes depending on the video length and complexity.

Violation Types

Lane Line Violations

Detects vehicles crossing solid lane lines or improperly changing lanes.

License Plate Issues

Identifies vehicles with missing, obscured, or unreadable license plates.

Illegal Line Crossing

Detects vehicles crossing stop lines or entering prohibited areas.

Not Yielding to Pedestrians

Identifies vehicles that fail to stop for pedestrians at zebra crossings.
